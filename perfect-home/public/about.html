    • Perfect Home is a card generator for the game "Welcome to Your Perfect Home", created by Benoit Turpin and produced by Blue Cocker Games and Deep Water Games.

    This application is intended to enable remote groups to play "Welcome to Your Perfect Home" together. It is not a complete implementation of the game, and can't be used for the full game-play experience without other game components that can be obtained from a licensed retailer for the distributors.
