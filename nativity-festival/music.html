    Music

    Music at the Peoria Area Community Festival of Nativities is provided by local professional, amateur, and student performers. If you would like to be considered to perform at 7th annual Peoria Area Community Festival of Nativities, provide your information and availability below. Musical selections should be arrangements of sacred Christmas music, but can be in any style or instrumentation. Musical acts will be considered as we receive them until our performance schedule has been filled. Please contact our music coordinator at pacfn_music@gmail.com with any questions.
